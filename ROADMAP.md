## üí° Design Principles

Throughout all improvements, maintain:
- **Faithful to Digimon/VPet aesthetics**: Keep the retro pixel art style
- **Simple, intuitive UI**: Don't overcomplicate the interface
- **Mobile-first**: All features must work well on touch devices
- **Optional complexity**: Advanced features shouldn't overwhelm new players
- **Respect player time**: No predatory mechanics or forced waiting
- **Community-driven**: Listen to player feedback for priorities
